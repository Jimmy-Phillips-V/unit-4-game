Rupee Raider

The Rules!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By click a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win by matching your total score to the random number; you will lose the game if your total score goes about the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, it will automatically change the value of each crystal.
